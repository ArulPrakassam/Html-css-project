Welcome to mobile Planetarium

MOBILE PLANETARIUM offers everything a traditional planetarium can, plus the benefit of extreme portability to travel to distant audiences. The very latest science and space shows, a 3D astronomical simulator, compatibility with well-known astronomical applications (e.g. Worldwide telescope, Stellarium) - everything is included in the MOBILE PLANETARIUM.

KEY FEATURES OF MOBILE PLANETARIUM

A COMPLETE PACKAGE

A complete solution including hardware, software, delivery, installation, training and content.

IPAD CONTROL

User-friendly iPad control makes system operation a cinch and frees the presenter to move around the dome and interact. with the audience.

FAST AND EASY SETUP

Dome set up is quick and easy, making short term installations possible. Calibration is fast and fully-automatic for perfect projection.

IMMERSIVE CONTENT PROVIDED

Includes hours of free educational content and easy access to hours more.

HIGHLY PORTABLE AND COMPACT

Domes and projection systems pack down into cases for easy transport. Small domes can be carried in a car, larger domes in a minivan.

CUSTOMIZABLE WEATHERPROOF OUTER

The outer shell of the dome can be customized with full color printing to increase your brand recognition.

SPECTACULAR IMAGE QUALITY

High-quality and bright projection exceeds the image quality of a typical, single channel projection system.

LOW MAINTENANCE COST

Single server and affordable components with hassle-free lamp replacement means low maintenance costs.
